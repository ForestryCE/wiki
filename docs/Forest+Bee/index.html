The Forest Bee (also as a Drone, a Princess, a Queen, and Larva) is one of the six Mundane Bee species in Forestry:CE. It is recognizeable by its light blue outline and yellow stripes. You can only obtain a Forest Princess and a Forest Drone from breaking a Forest Hive with a Scoop

It is the most common pest in forests. The Forest Bee, as well as the Meadow Bee, are both known to be docile, not causing people even the smallest of stings. - Forester's Manual "Mundane Bees"

Products

Forest Bees are very ordinary with their produce. Each work cycle they have a 30% (9% accounted for speed) chance to produce a Honey Comb. With default traits, in a Bee House, they are estimated to live about 0:09:07 of real-world time; during their life (20 cycles) they can produce an average of 1.8 Honey Combs.
